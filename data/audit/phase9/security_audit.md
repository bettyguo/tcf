## Authz fuzzer coverage

`tests/integration/test_authz_fuzz.py` enumerates every
`/v1/.../{id}` route declared in `docs/api/openapi.v1.yaml` and
verifies user-B cannot read/write user-A's resources. Coverage
spot-checked by Reviewer 2 against the OpenAPI spec on 2026-05-28;
no route uncovered.

## PII logging hygiene

`tests/unit/test_logging_no_pii.py` feeds a mock submission through
the API, captures the log emitter, and asserts no candidate text /
audio path / email / phone appears in the captured records. Passing
at v1.0.
