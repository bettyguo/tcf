  <p style="margin-top:12px; color:var(--ink-soft); font-size:var(--fs-sm);">
    The index <code><a href="{{ '/search-index.json' | relative_url }}">/search-index.json</a></code> is generated by Jekyll at build time — one JSON entry per published page, containing the title, URL, an excerpt, and the first 4000 chars of body text (HTML stripped). A scoring function (term-frequency × inverse-page-frequency, capped) ranks pages; phrase matches and title hits get a bonus. Snippets are extracted around the strongest match. All execution is local to your browser. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking pixel.
